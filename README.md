        <h2>The Market Opportunity</h2>
        <p>A significant barrier to entry in the stock market is financial literacy. Our research identified a key challenge:</p>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Knowledge Gap:</strong> Approximately <strong>76% of individuals in India</strong> lack a clear understanding of personal finance and investment vehicles, leading to hesitation and missed opportunities for wealth creation.</li>
            <li><strong>Uncertainty & Risk:</strong> The inherent volatility of the market, coupled with a lack of reliable predictive tools, discourages potential investors.</li>
        </ul>
    </div>

    <div class="highlight-box">
        <strong>Core Innovation:</strong> Our pivotal differentiation lies in leveraging <strong>Fundamental Analysis</strong>. Unlike traditional machine learning algorithms that rely solely on historical stock prices, our model incorporates core financial health indicators such as profitability ratios, return on taxes, and other key business metrics for a more accurate and holistic prediction.
    </div>

    <div class="section">
        <h2>Our Solution: A Unified Platform</h2>
        <p>We have developed an accessible and intelligent system that integrates essential financial planning tools into a single, user-friendly platform. Key features include:</p>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Predictive Forecasting:</strong> Projects future stock values with higher accuracy, minimizing prediction errors and overcoming market uncertainty.</li>
            <li><strong>Integrated Risk Profiling:</strong> Combines individual risk assessment with data-driven stock forecasting for personalized investment strategies.</li>
            <li><strong>Data-Centric Recommendations:</strong> Provides clear, actionable recommendations based on comprehensive financial analysis.</li>
            <li><strong>User-Centric Design:</strong> An accessible system designed to empower both novice and experienced investors in their financial journey.</li>
